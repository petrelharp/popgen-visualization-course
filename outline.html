Visualizing geographic structure and demographic history

Goals/skills: 1. Describe different causes and patterns of population structure. 2. Use principal components analysis (PCA) to visualize population structure. 3. Use BEDASSLE to fit models of isolation by distance and/or environment.

Incidental skills: 1. Interpreting the output of a Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) algorithm.

Prerequisites: 1. Install R 2. and the R package BEDASSLE: {r} install.packages("BEDASSLE")
